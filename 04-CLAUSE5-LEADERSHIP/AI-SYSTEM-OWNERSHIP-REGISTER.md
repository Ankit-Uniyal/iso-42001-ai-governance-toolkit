## Owner Responsibilities

Each AI System Owner is accountable for:

1. **Risk Identification** — Identifying and reporting risks associated with their AI system
2. **Impact Assessments** — Commissioning and approving AI System Impact Assessments (Clause 8.2)
3. **Lifecycle Management** — Ensuring the system follows the AI Lifecycle Management Procedure
4. **Performance Monitoring** — Tracking system performance and flagging anomalies
5. **Incident Reporting** — Reporting AI incidents in line with the Incident Response Procedure
6. **Supplier Oversight** — Where third-party AI is used, managing supplier relationships
7. **Documentation** — Keeping system cards, model documentation, and operational records current
8. **Compliance** — Ensuring their system complies with applicable regulations and AIMS controls
