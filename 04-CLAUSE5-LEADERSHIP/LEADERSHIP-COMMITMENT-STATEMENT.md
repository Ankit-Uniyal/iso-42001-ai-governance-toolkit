## Statement of Leadership Commitment to Responsible AI

---

[ORGANISATION NAME]
Leadership Commitment to the AI Management System (AIMS)

---

We, the senior leadership of [Organisation Name], hereby commit to the following in relation to our AI Management System (AIMS), implemented in accordance with ISO/IEC 42001:2023:

**1. Strategic Alignment**
We commit to ensuring that our AI Management System is aligned with our organisational strategy and that responsible AI is embedded into our business direction. AI governance is not a compliance exercise — it is a business imperative.

**2. Policy and Objectives**
We have established an AI Management Policy that reflects our values and commitments. We ensure that AI objectives are set, communicated, and reviewed at appropriate levels across the organisation.

**3. Resource Provision**
We commit to providing the financial, human, and technological resources necessary to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve the AIMS. AI governance will be adequately funded and staffed.

**4. Communication**
We commit to communicating the importance of responsible AI management across the organisation and to ensuring that all staff understand their role in the AIMS.

**5. Continual Improvement**
We commit to continually improving the suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the AIMS through regular management review, internal audit, and active monitoring.

**6. Accountability**
We accept ultimate accountability for AI governance within this organisation. We have appointed an AI Governance Lead with the authority and resources to manage the AIMS on a day-to-day basis.

**7. Ethical AI**
We commit to the ethical development and use of AI — including fairness, transparency, accountability, privacy, and human oversight as core principles of our AI programmes.
