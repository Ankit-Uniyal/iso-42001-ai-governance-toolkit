## Role Descriptions Summary

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| CEO / Top Management | Ultimate accountability for AIMS; approves policy and resources |
| AI Governance Lead / CAIO | Day-to-day AIMS management; owns most AIMS processes |
| AI System Owner | Accountable for specific AI systems in scope |
| AI Developer / MLOps | Technical implementation; model documentation; bias testing |
| DPO | Data Privacy Officer; AI + personal data compliance interface |
| Risk Manager | AI risk register; treatment plans; monitoring oversight |
| Internal Auditor | AIMS internal audits; independent of areas audited |
| HR / People Lead | Competence, training, awareness programmes |
| Legal | Regulatory compliance; contractual controls; AI disclosures |
