## Purpose

This log records all planned and implemented changes to the AI Management System, its processes, scope, policies, and controls. It ensures that changes are made in a planned, controlled, and documented way, as required by Clause 6.3.

---

## Change Triggers

Changes to the AIMS must be documented when triggered by:

- New AI system being brought into scope
- Major update to an existing in-scope AI system (new model version, new training data, new use case)
- New or amended regulation or legal requirement
- Significant organisational change (merger, acquisition, restructure, new product line)
- Internal audit finding requiring systemic AIMS change
- Management review decision
- External audit / certification finding
- Risk assessment identifying a new systemic gap
