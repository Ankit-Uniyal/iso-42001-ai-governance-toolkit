## 6.1.2 — AI Risk Assessment

Establish a documented, repeatable process. Use AI-RISK-ASSESSMENT-PROCESS.md for the process and AI-RISK-REGISTER.md to record assessed risks.

## 6.1.3 — AI Risk Treatment

Select: Accept / Mitigate / Transfer / Avoid. Compare chosen controls against all Annex A controls. Produce Statement of Applicability. Get risk owner sign-off. See RISK-TREATMENT-PLAN.md and STATEMENT-OF-APPLICABILITY.md.

## 6.2 — AI Objectives

Establish measurable objectives with owner, deadline, and KPIs. See AI-OBJECTIVES-REGISTER.md.

## 6.3 — Planning of Changes

Log all planned AIMS changes with impact assessment. See AIMS-CHANGE-LOG.md.
