## Purpose

This document records the treatment decisions and planned actions for all AI risks that require treatment, as identified in the AI Risk Register (AI-RISK-REGISTER.md). It satisfies the requirements of Clause 6.1.3.

---

## Treatment Options

| Option | Description | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Avoid** | Eliminate the risk by stopping the activity | Risk score is Critical and treatment is not feasible or proportionate |
| **Reduce** | Implement controls to lower likelihood or impact | Most common option; implement specific technical or procedural controls |
| **Transfer** | Shift risk to third party (e.g., insurance, contractual liability) | Where external party is better placed to manage the risk |
| **Accept** | Acknowledge the risk and monitor without treatment | Risk is Low/Medium and within risk appetite; residual risk accepted by management |
