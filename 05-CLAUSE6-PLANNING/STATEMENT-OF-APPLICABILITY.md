## Purpose

This Statement of Applicability (SoA) is a mandatory document required by ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Clause 6.1.3. It documents for each Annex A control:

1. Whether the control is **applicable** to the organization
2. The **justification** for inclusion or exclusion
3. The **implementation status** of applicable controls
4. The **reference** to where the control is implemented

The SoA must be reviewed and updated when:
- The AIMS scope changes
- New AI systems are brought into scope
- Risk assessments identify new risks requiring additional controls
- Previously excluded controls become relevant
- At minimum, annually as part of management review

---

## How to Complete This SoA

**Applicability (A/NA):** Mark each control as Applicable (A) or Not Applicable (NA)

**Justification for Exclusion:** For NA controls, document the reason (e.g., "Organization does not develop AI systems — only deploys third-party AI"; "No AI projects with external supplier involvement")

**Implementation Status:**
- **Implemented** — Control is fully in place and operating effectively
- **Partially Implemented** — Control is partially in place; gap remediation in progress
- **Planned** — Control is not yet implemented; implementation planned
- **Not Implemented** — Control is applicable but not yet implemented (represents a gap)
