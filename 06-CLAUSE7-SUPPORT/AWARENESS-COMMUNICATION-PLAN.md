## Part 1 — Awareness Programme (Clause 7.3)

All in-scope persons must be aware of: the AI Policy and their contribution to it; their role in AIMS effectiveness; the consequences of non-conformity; and how to report AI concerns.

### Awareness Activities

| Activity | Content | Audience | Channel | Frequency | Owner |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|-----------|-------|
| AIMS Policy Launch | Full AI Policy, purpose, commitments | All staff | CEO email + intranet | At launch | AI Gov Lead / CEO |
| Annual AI Ethics E-Learning | AI policy, ethics, reporting obligations | All in-scope staff | LMS | Annual | HR |
| AI Incident Reporting Briefing | How to identify and report incidents | All staff | Briefing + reference card | At launch + updates | AI Gov Lead |
| New Employee AI Onboarding | AI policy, AIMS scope, their role | New hires | Induction | At hire | HR |
| AI Governance Team Workshop | Full AIMS requirements, roles | AI Gov, System Owners, Developers | Workshop | Annual | AI Gov Lead |
| Management AI Governance Update | AIMS status, key risks, objectives | Senior Management | Board pack | Quarterly | AI Gov Lead |
| Policy Update Notifications | Changes to AI policy or procedures | All affected staff | Email + intranet | On change | AI Gov Lead |

### Awareness Metrics

| Metric | Target | Method | Frequency | Owner |
|--------|--------|--------|-----------|-------|
| % of in-scope staff completing AI ethics training | 90% | LMS report | Annual | HR |
| % of staff who know how to report an AI incident | 80% | Survey | Annual | AI Gov Lead |
| % of staff who can identify the AI Governance Lead | 70% | Survey | Annual | AI Gov Lead |
