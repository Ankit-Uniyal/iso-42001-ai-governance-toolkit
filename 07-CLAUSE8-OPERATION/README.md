## 8.1 — Operational Planning and Control

The organisation must plan, implement, control, and maintain the processes needed to meet requirements for the provision of AI systems, and to implement the actions determined in Clause 6.

Key requirements:
- Establish criteria for processes and acceptance of AI systems
- - Control processes in accordance with criteria
  - - Keep documented evidence that processes were carried out as planned
    - - Control planned changes and review unintended changes
     
      - See: OPERATIONAL-CONTROLS-REGISTER.md, AI-LIFECYCLE-MANAGEMENT-PROCEDURE.md, AI-DEPLOYMENT-CHECKLIST.md, AI-CHANGE-CONTROL-PROCEDURE.md
     
      - ## 8.2 — AI System Impact Assessment
     
      - Conduct an impact assessment before deploying AI systems to identify potential harms and required mitigations. See AI-SYSTEM-IMPACT-ASSESSMENT.md.
     
      - ## 8.3 — AI System Objectives and Design
     
      - Document AI system objectives, design choices, and performance requirements. See AI-MODEL-CARD-TEMPLATE.md.
     
      - ## 8.4 — AI Suppliers and Third Parties
     
      - Assess and monitor all third-party AI suppliers for risk. See AI-SUPPLIER-ASSESSMENT.md and AI-SUPPLIER-RISK-REGISTER.md.
