## Purpose and ISO 42001 Compliance

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Clause 9.3 requires top management to review the AIMS at planned intervals to ensure its continuing suitability, adequacy, effectiveness, and alignment with the strategic direction of the organization.

**Minimum frequency:** Annually (more frequent reviews recommended during initial implementation and for high-risk AI deployments)
